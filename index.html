Why Us?

1. I don't keep your device while replacement parts are being delivered

2. You choose my commision based on my performance and only have to pay for replacement parts

3. You can choose to cancel the repair process at any point

4. You can watch and be a part of every step along the way

Anything I Should Know?

1. You have to sign a release of liability waiver before repairs can begin

2. If you choose to purchase the parts and not continue with the repair there are no refunds

My Process

1. I determine the issue with you, the customer

2. The replacement parts are ordered and paid for upfront

3. You are notified when the parts arrive and bring in the device

4. You sign a release of liability waiver

5. I begin the repairs on video with you allowed to watch

6. I return device to you, upload the video, and let you choose my commision with no obligation for any pay
